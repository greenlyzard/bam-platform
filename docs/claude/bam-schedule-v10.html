🔒 Secure enrollment · Cancellation & Billing Terms
Enrollment Terms

Billing

  • Monthly tuition is automatically billed on the 15th of each month.
  • Your first class is complimentary — you will not be charged until you formally enroll.
  • Tuition covers the full calendar month regardless of absences.
  • Unlimited Classes plan: $249/month, auto-billed on the 15th.

Cancellation

  • 30 days written notice is required to cancel enrollment.
  • Notice must be submitted via email to dance@bamsocal.com or in writing at the front desk.
  • Cancellations mid-month do not result in a prorated refund for the current billing period.

Makeups & Absences

  • One makeup class per month may be scheduled within the same season with advance notice.
  • Makeups must be in an equivalent level class and are subject to availability.

Questions

Contact us at dance@bamsocal.com or (949) 229-0846.

Class Placement Evaluation
We want every dancer to start in exactly the right class. Our complimentary placement evaluation is a brief one-on-one session with a BAM instructor to assess your background, technique, and goals.
What to Expect

A 15–20 minute informal session at the studio. You will move through a few exercises with an instructor — nothing to prepare, just come as you are. Completely low-pressure.
