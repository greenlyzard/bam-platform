## Marketing Intelligence: What Parents Actually Buy

Based on research across dance studio marketing data:

**Parents don't buy dance classes. They buy:**
- Confidence for their child
- Structure and discipline
- A nurturing environment
- A community they belong to
- The feeling that their child is getting something special

**Top reasons parents choose a studio:**
1. Word of mouth / referral from trusted friend (88% trust rate)
2. Google search + reviews (first thing they see)
3. Social media — seeing real content from real students
4. Website — professional, easy to understand, easy to register
5. Trial class — if they show up, 70%+ convert if experience is good

**Top reasons parents leave a studio:**
1. Child doesn't feel seen/recognized
2. Communication is poor
3. Registration/payment friction
4. Studio culture feels competitive or negative
5. Teacher turnover

**BAM's platform addresses every one of these directly.**
