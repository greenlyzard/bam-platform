## Level Number System (Age Brackets)

| Level Number | Age Range | Description |
|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| Petites | 3–5 years | Pre-technique, creative movement and imaginative play |
| 1 | 5–7 years | Foundational technique, first formal dance training |
| 2 | 7–10 years | Building structured technique across disciplines |
| 3 | 10–13 years | Refining and advancing technique toward company level |
| 4 | 13+ years | Company-level, pre-professional, performance focused |
| Adult/Teen | 14+ years | All levels, non-competitive, welcoming environment |

## Level Letter System (Skill Within Age Group)

| Letter | Skill | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| A | Beginner | New to formal technique in this age group |
| B | Intermediate | Solid foundation, advancing combinations |
| C | Advanced | Near-company level, competition-eligible |

**Examples:**
- Level 2A = Ages 7–10, Beginner — first formal ballet/jazz classes
- Level 3B = Ages 10–13, Intermediate — advancing turns, leaps, performance
- Level 4C = Ages 13+, Advanced — full company training, pointe, repertoire

**Placement:** When in doubt, we recommend a free assessment class. Contact us at
dance@bamsocal.com or (949) 229-0846.
