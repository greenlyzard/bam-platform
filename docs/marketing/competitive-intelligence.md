# Competitive Intelligence
## Ballet Academy and Movement — South Orange County Dance Studio Landscape

> **Purpose:** Track competitor positioning, pricing, programs, and marketing style to inform BAM's differentiation strategy.  
> **Update frequency:** Quarterly, or when significant competitor changes are observed.

---

## 1. Competitive Segments

South Orange County dance studios fall into three distinct segments:

| Segment | Description | BAM Relationship |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Classical Ballet** | Curriculum-driven, technique-first, professional track | Direct competitors |
| **Competition Dance** | Competition teams, commercial style, jazz/hip-hop heavy | Different market (but competes for age 5-10 families) |
| **Recreational / Franchise** | Open enrollment, low-commitment, franchise models | Competes for first-time families ages 3-5 |

---

## 2. Direct Competitors — Classical Ballet

### Southland Ballet Academy
- **Segment:** Classical ballet
- **Location:** Aliso Viejo area
- **Programs:** Ballet, Pointe, variations
- **Class sizes:** Unknown
- **Pricing:** Track quarterly
- **Teachers:** Track for recruitment opportunities
- **Marketing style:** Traditional, credential-focused
- **Differentiator vs. BAM:** More established in north OC; less presence in San Clemente market
- **Threat level:** Medium
- **Notes:** Direct comp for families in Laguna Niguel / Mission Viejo

---

### Pacific Ballet Conservatory
- **Segment:** Classical ballet / conservatory
- **Location:** South OC
- **Programs:** Ballet training, performance opportunities
- **Class sizes:** Unknown
- **Pricing:** Track quarterly
- **Marketing style:** Conservatory positioning, academic tone
- **Differentiator vs. BAM:** Higher price point possible; less nurturing tone
- **Threat level:** Medium
- **Notes:** Competes for serious ballet families; less overlap on ages 3-6

---

### San Clemente Dance Academy
- **Segment:** Recreational + Performance + Competition (3-track model)
- **Location:** San Clemente (direct geography competitor)
- **Programs:** Ballet, jazz, hip-hop, competition team
- **Structure:** 3-track — recreational / performance / competition
- **Marketing style:** High-energy, achievement-focused
- **Differentiator vs. BAM:** Multi-genre; less classical focus; more commercial
- **Threat level:** HIGH (same city, overlapping age range)
- **Notes:** Closest structural competitor in San Clemente. Watch carefully.

---

### Capistrano Academy of Dance
- **Segment:** Ballet + commercial
- **Location:** San Juan Capistrano area
- **Programs:** Ballet, jazz, contemp, acro
- **Marketing style:** Family-friendly, community-focused
- **Differentiator vs. BAM:** Broader genre mix; less classical emphasis
- **Threat level:** Medium
- **Notes:** Established in SJC corridor; relevant for expansion research

---

### South Coast Conservatory
- **Segment:** Conservatory model — higher price point
- **Location:** South OC
- **Programs:** Conservatory-track dance and performing arts
- **Pricing:** Premium
- **Marketing style:** Academic, achievement-focused
- **Differentiator vs. BAM:** Different price tier; less accessible for general family market
- **Threat level:** Low (different customer)
- **Notes:** May attract the same top-performing students for intensives

## 4. Recreational / Franchise Studios

### Tutu School
- **Segment:** Franchise toddler ballet
- **Ages:** 2-6 primarily
- **Model:** Franchise — standardized curriculum
- **Pricing:** Mid-range
- **Differentiator vs. BAM:** Franchise quality ceiling; less professional instruction
- **Threat level:** Medium — directly competes for first-time ballet families ages 2-5
- **Notes:** Parents who start at Tutu School often graduate to a real studio around age 5-6. BAM should position as "the next step."

---

### Bonjour Ballet
- **Segment:** Franchise toddler ballet (French-inspired branding)
- **Ages:** 2-6 primarily
- **Model:** Franchise
- **Differentiator vs. BAM:** Lifestyle brand appeal; limited technique depth
- **Threat level:** Medium — same age range competition
- **Notes:** Same "graduate-to-BAM" opportunity as Tutu School

## 8. Intelligence Sources

To gather competitor data:
1. Visit competitor websites quarterly
2. Follow competitor Instagram accounts (admin account, not personal)
3. Google their studio name to see reviews and any press
4. Check Yelp listings for pricing info
5. Ask incoming students what studios they considered
6. Watch for teacher movement (LinkedIn, Instagram)
