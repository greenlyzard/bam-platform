# Location Expansion Strategy
## Ballet Academy and Movement — Studio Location 2

> **Goal:** Identify the optimal location for BAM's second studio, determine readiness indicators, and create a pre-launch framework.  
> **Timeline:** Location 2 target — evaluate readiness by late 2026; open by 2027 if indicators met.

---

## 2. Target City Analysis

### Primary Candidates

#### Ladera Ranch
- **Population:** ~23,000 (master-planned community)
- **Demographics:** Predominantly upper-middle to high income families; young children heavily represented
- **Family profile:** Exactly BAM's target market — dual-income households, education-focused, arts-appreciative
- **Competitor presence:** Low classical ballet representation — significant market gap
- **Distance from San Clemente:** ~18 miles north (Ortega Highway / Antonio Pkwy corridor)
- **Real estate:** Commercial space in Ladera Ranch Town Center and surrounding retail
- **Verdict:** **Strong candidate** — demographics match perfectly, low competition, high income concentration

#### Rancho Mission Viejo
- **Population:** Growing master-planned community (target 14,000 homes at buildout)
- **Demographics:** Similar to Ladera Ranch — planned for young families, high income
- **Competitor presence:** Minimal currently — early-mover advantage possible
- **Distance from San Clemente:** ~12 miles north
- **Real estate:** Commercial space developing along Sendero and Esencia Village areas
- **Verdict:** **Strong candidate** — fastest-growing family community in South OC; get there first

#### San Juan Capistrano
- **Population:** ~35,000
- **Demographics:** Mixed income; established community; some family concentration near schools
- **Competitor presence:** Capistrano Academy of Dance is established here
- **Distance from San Clemente:** ~6 miles north (very close)
- **Risk:** Geographic overlap with San Clemente — could cannibalize rather than expand
- **Verdict:** **Lower priority** — too close to Location 1; competitor already established

---

### Secondary Candidates

#### Mission Viejo
- **Population:** ~95,000 (largest city in South OC)
- **Demographics:** Established, upper-middle income, significant family population
- **Competitor presence:** Multiple studios already operating
- **Distance from San Clemente:** ~20 miles north
- **Verdict:** **Medium priority** — large market but more competitive; evaluate after Ladera/RMV

#### Laguna Niguel
- **Population:** ~68,000
- **Demographics:** High income, family-heavy, arts-engaged
- **Competitor presence:** Some competition
- **Distance from San Clemente:** ~12 miles north
- **Verdict:** **Medium priority** — solid demographics but less obvious gap than Ladera/RMV

## 3. Location 2 Evaluation Framework

When evaluating a specific space, score these factors:

| Factor | Weight | Scoring Criteria |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Demographics (family concentration, income) | 25% | 3+ schools within 2 miles; median HH income $100k+ |
| Competition density | 20% | No classical ballet within 3 miles = 10pts; one competitor = 6pts; two+ = 2pts |
| Real estate quality | 20% | Ground floor, high-visibility, parking, 1,500+ sq ft for 2 classrooms |
| Distance from Location 1 | 15% | 10-20 miles ideal; too close cannibalizes; too far is hard to manage |
| Lease terms | 10% | 3-year initial term; personal guarantee risk assessment |
| Growth trajectory | 10% | Is the neighborhood growing (new homes, schools) or plateauing? |

## 4. Space Requirements — Location 2

| Requirement | Spec |
|-------------|------|
| Total square footage | 1,500-2,500 sq ft |

| Storage | Costume and prop storage (100+ sq ft) |
| Parking | Minimum 10 dedicated spaces; or shared lot |
| Visibility | Ground floor, street-facing, or anchor-adjacent in retail center |

## 5. Staffing Model for Location 2

Location 2 requires the following minimum staffing before opening:

| Role | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Lead Instructor | 1 full-time (or very committed part-time) capable of running the studio independently |
| Assistant Instructor | 1 part-time for coverage |
| Studio Admin / Front Desk | 1 part-time (can share with Location 1 for first year) |
| Amanda Cobb | Split time between both locations — teaches 2-3 days/week at Location 2 initially |

**Critical:** Do not open Location 2 without a lead instructor who can run it independently. Amanda cannot be in two places every day.
